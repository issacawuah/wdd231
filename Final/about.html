About Sunyani Food Trucks

Connecting food lovers with the best mobile dining experiences in Sunyani, Ghana

Our Mission

To promote and support local food truck entrepreneurs while providing food lovers with easy access to diverse, delicious, and authentic culinary experiences throughout Sunyani.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform that celebrates and grows Sunyani's vibrant food truck culture, fostering community connections through the universal language of great food.

Our Story

Sunyani Food Truck Directory was born from a simple observation: the incredible diversity and quality of mobile food vendors in our beloved city wasn't getting the recognition it deserved.

Founded in 2024, we set out to create a comprehensive platform that would not only showcase these culinary entrepreneurs but also make it easier for residents and visitors to discover new flavors and support local businesses.

Today, we're proud to feature over 25 food trucks serving everything from traditional Ghanaian dishes to innovative fusion cuisine, all while maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity.

Our Values

Community First

We believe in building strong community connections through food and supporting local entrepreneurs.

Quality Assurance

We maintain high standards and only feature food trucks that meet our quality and safety requirements.

Authenticity

We celebrate genuine flavors and authentic culinary traditions from Ghana and around the world.

Inclusivity

We welcome and support food trucks of all sizes, cuisines, and backgrounds.
