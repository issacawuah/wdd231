Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register my food truck?

To register your food truck, please fill out our contact form with the subject "Food Truck Registration" or email us directly at info@sunyanifoodtrucks.com. We'll guide you through the process and requirements.

What are the requirements for listing?

We require valid business registration, food safety certificates, and high-quality photos of your food truck and menu items. All trucks must meet local health and safety standards.

How do I update my truck information?

Registered food truck owners can update their information through our partner portal or by contacting our support team. We recommend keeping your menu and location information current.

Is there a fee for listing?

Basic listings are free for all food trucks. We also offer premium features and enhanced visibility options for a small monthly fee.
