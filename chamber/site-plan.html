Sunyani Food Truck Directory

Site Plan & Development Blueprint

Developer: Awuah Isaac

Site Name

Sunyani Food Truck Directory

Reason for Selection: This name clearly identifies the geographic location (Sunyani, Ghana) and the specific service (Food Truck Directory). It's descriptive, memorable, and immediately communicates the site's purpose to visitors. The name emphasizes the local focus while highlighting the directory aspect that helps users discover mobile dining options.

Optional Domain: sunyani-foodtrucks.gh

Site Purpose

The Sunyani Food Truck Directory serves as a comprehensive online platform that connects food lovers with mobile dining experiences throughout Sunyani, Ghana. The site provides:

  • A centralized directory of local food trucks with detailed information
  • Search and filtering capabilities to find specific cuisines or locations
  • Food truck profiles including menus, operating hours, and locations
  • User reviews and ratings to help with food discovery decisions
  • Contact information and social media links for food truck owners
  • Information about local food culture and Ghanaian cuisine
  • A platform for food truck owners to showcase their businesses

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Local Resident Looking for Dinner

Question: "I'm hungry and want to try something new for dinner. What food trucks are operating near me tonight, and which ones have good reviews for traditional Ghanaian dishes?"

Content Need: Search functionality, location-based filtering, cuisine categories, operating hours, and user reviews.

Scenario 2: Tourist Exploring Local Cuisine

Question: "I'm visiting Sunyani and want to experience authentic local food. Which food trucks serve the best jollof rice and kelewele, and where can I find them?"

Content Need: Cultural food information, specialty dish listings, tourist-friendly descriptions, and location maps.

Scenario 3: Food Truck Owner Seeking Visibility

Question: "I own a food truck in Sunyani and want more customers to find me. How can I list my truck on this directory and showcase my menu?"

Content Need: Business registration form, profile creation tools, and promotional opportunities.

Color Usage:

  • Primary Orange (#FF6B35): Call-to-action buttons, food images, highlights, and warm accents representing energy and appetite
  • Secondary Green (#2C5530): Headers, navigation, footer, and main branding representing Ghana's natural beauty and growth
  • Accent Sand (#F4A261): Background elements, cards, and subtle highlights representing warmth and hospitality
  • Dark Gray (#2C2C2C): Body text and main content for readability
  • White (#FFFFFF): Background areas and text on dark backgrounds
