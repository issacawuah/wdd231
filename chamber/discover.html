Must-See Attractions

Explore Ghana's most iconic destinations

Cape Coast Castle

Central Region 2-3 hours

UNESCO World Heritage site with profound historical significance. Explore the dark history of the transatlantic slave trade and enjoy stunning ocean views.

History UNESCO Coastal

Kakum National Park

Central Region Full day

Walk among the treetops on the famous canopy walkway. Experience Ghana's rainforest with its rich biodiversity and unique flora and fauna.

Nature Adventure Wildlife

Mole National Park

Northern Region 2-3 days

Ghana's largest wildlife refuge. Spot elephants, antelopes, and over 300 bird species on safari walks and guided tours.

Safari Wildlife Adventure

Lake Volta

Volta Region Full day

The world's largest man-made lake by surface area. Enjoy boat tours, fishing expeditions, and visits to Dodi Island.

Lake Boating Scenic

Kwame Nkrumah Mausoleum

Greater Accra 1-2 hours

Memorial park dedicated to Ghana's first president and prominent Pan-Africanist. A significant landmark in Ghana's history.

History Landmark Cultural

Wli Waterfalls

Volta Region Half day

The highest waterfall in West Africa. A breathtaking natural wonder surrounded by lush rainforest and diverse wildlife.

Nature Waterfall Hiking

Elmina Castle

Central Region 2 hours

Oldest European building in sub-Saharan Africa. A poignant historical site with guided tours that tell powerful stories.

History UNESCO Coastal

Kumasi Cultural Center

Ashanti Region 3-4 hours

Vibrant marketplace and cultural hub showcasing Ashanti crafts, traditions, and performing arts. A must-visit for cultural enthusiasts.

Culture Shopping Arts
